Electronics Tips

AC Maintenance Tips:

  • Always clean the filter regularly.
  • Keep the AC coils clean for better cooling.
  • Check for any leakage and get it repaired.

Refrigerator Tips:

  • Ensure the door seals are tight to save energy.
  • Defrost regularly to maintain efficiency.
  • Keep the refrigerator clean to prevent bad odors.

Washing Machine Tips:

  • Clean the drum and filter regularly.
  • Balance the load to prevent damage.
  • Use the right detergent for the best wash quality.
